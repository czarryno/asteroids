Asteroids

  • Press the Up arrow key or W to thrust forward, Press the left/right arrow keys, or A/D, to angle the rocket that way
  • Press space bar to shoot
  • Press R to Warp! The cooldown on this is around 20 seconds
  • Warping has a short invincibility buff afterwards
  • If you get hit by an asteroid there is a short cooldown period where the player is invincible.
         Be careful not to get caught in an asteroid after the timer runs out!
